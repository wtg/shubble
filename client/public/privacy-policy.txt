Privacy Policy
Effective Date: 10-03-2025

Shubble (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect
The App may access your device location in order to:

Show you the closest shuttle stop.
Calculate how far you are from that stop.

We do not collect, store, or transmit your location data or any other personal information. All processing happens locally on your device.

Use of Information
Location data is used only in real time to display shuttle stop information.

Your location data is never stored, logged, or sent to our servers.

Sharing of Information
We do not share your location or any other personal information with third parties.

Children’s Privacy
Shubble does not knowingly collect, store, or share personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy
If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update it here and revise the “Effective Date” above.
